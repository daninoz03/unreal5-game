Through exploration and discovery, Kira learns that this realm was once home to the **Sylvan Guardians**, a civilization that lived in harmony with nature's magic.

## Themes

### Primary Theme: **Legacy & Heritage**
What do we inherit from those who came before? Kira inherits both her grandmother's curiosity and a responsibility she never knew existed. The forest inherits the consequences of the Guardians' choices.

### Secondary Theme: **Balance & Harmony**
The Guardians lived in balance with nature. Their departure broke that balance. The Blight isn't an invading forceâ€”it's what happens when balance is lost.

### Tertiary Theme: **Restoration vs. Preservation**
Can we restore what was lost, or only preserve what remains? The ending explores whether the forest can truly be saved, or only given a chance to heal on its own.

*"More followed Lyric into the outside world. Each departure was a wound. The elders warned us: the Heart cannot survive alone. But the young ones dreamed of distant lands."*

### Lore Stone #6: The Council's Debate
*"Do we force those who left to return? Do we seal the boundary forever? The Council argued for decades while the Blight spread. In the end, we could agree on nothing."*

### Lore Stone #7: The Exodus
*"One by one, the remaining Guardians left. Some to find the others. Some to escape the dying forest. Some because they lost hope. Soon, only a handful remained."*
