## Area Breakdown

### 1. The Threshold
**Purpose**: Dramatic transition, establish surreal tone

**Description**:  
The boundary between the material ruins and the Heart's domain. As Kira crosses, reality visibly shiftsâ€”colors saturate, geometry softens, time seems to flow strangely.

**Visual Effects**:
- Gradual color shift (muted â†’ vibrant with corruption streaks)
- Floating particles (light and dark intermingled)
- Occasional "glitches" where past and present overlap
- Sound distortion

**Key Elements**:
- Checkpoint (point of no return feeling)
- Kira's dialogue about the strangeness
- Vista of the Heart visible through trees (goal clear)
- Path splits into two routes

**Environmental Details**:
- Flowers blooming and dying in cycles
- Trees shifting between healthy and corrupted states
- Ghost Echoes more frequent, more vivid
- The forest "watching" the player

## Final Boss: The Blighted Heart

### Boss Overview
The Blighted Heart isn't a creatureâ€”it's the forest's pain given form. It doesn't attack maliciously; it lashes out because it's been alone and suffering for centuries.

### Health Phases
- **Total Health**: 500 HP
- **Phase 1**: 500-350 HP (Learning phase)
- **Phase 2**: 350-150 HP (Intensified)
- **Phase 3**: 150-0 HP (Desperate)

### Attack Patterns

**Phase 1: Sorrow (500-350 HP)**
| Attack | Telegraph | Damage | Counter |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| Root Slam | 2.5 sec ground glow | 30 HP | Dodge away |
| Corruption Wave | 2 sec gathering darkness | 25 HP | Jump over |
| Tendril Swipe | 1.5 sec wind-up | 20 HP | Dodge toward |

**Vulnerability**: After Root Slam, core glows brieflyâ€”ATTACK WINDOW (3 seconds)

**Phase 2: Anger (350-150 HP)**
| Attack | Telegraph | Damage | Counter |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| All Phase 1 (faster) | -0.5 sec | +5 HP | Same |
| Corruption Eruption | Ground cracks | 35 HP | Run to edge |
| Dark Pulse | 3 sec charge | 40 HP | Hide behind root |

**Vulnerability**: After Dark Pulse, exhaustedâ€”ATTACK WINDOW (4 seconds)

**Phase 3: Desperation (150-0 HP)**
| Attack | Telegraph | Damage | Counter |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| All Previous (erratic) | Variable | Variable | React quickly |
| Memory Assault | Screen flashes | 0 HP | Can't dodge (story) |
| Final Slam | Long charge | 50 HP | Time your dodge |

**Memory Assault**: Visions flashâ€”the forest's memories, its pain. Not damage, but disorienting. Shows Kira what the Heart has endured.

**Final Vulnerability**: After long charge, completely vulnerableâ€”END THE FIGHT

### Boss Arena Dynamics

**Shifting States**:
- Arena alternates between "Light" and "Dark" states
- Light State: Health pickups appear, corruption pools shrink
- Dark State: More damage, pools expand, attacks intensify

**Root Cover**:
- Four root formations provide temporary cover
- Each can absorb 2-3 attacks before being destroyed
- Regenerate during Light State

### Victory Sequence

**Combat Ends**:
1. Final blow staggers the Heart
2. Corruption visibly recedes
3. Core becomes accessible
4. Player approaches (automatic)
5. Cutscene triggers: Kira touches the Heart

**The Visions**:
- Player experiences the Heart's memories
- The forest in its glory
- The slow abandonment
- The loneliness and pain
- A plea, not for revenge, but for connection

**The Choice** (for this demo, choice is presented but one path plays):
- Restore: Become the Guardian
- Release: Free the remaining energy

**Resolution**:
- Corruption fades
- Forest begins to heal
- Kira transformed (subtle visual change)
- Walk through restored grove
- Ghost Echoes welcome her
- Credits/End title

## Developer Notes

1. **The boss is the game's thesis**: Everything leads here. It must feel earned.
2. **Emotional weight matters**: This isn't just a fightâ€”it's a connection.
3. **Don't rush the ending**: Let the resolution breathe.
4. **The visions are crucial**: Show, don't tell, the Heart's suffering.
5. **Surreal doesn't mean confusing**: Player should always know where to go.
6. **Test the boss extensively**: This fight will make or break the experience.
